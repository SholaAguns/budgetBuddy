Connect your bank accounts to automatically import transactions

Setup Required: To use bank account integration, you need GoCardless API credentials.
  1. Sign up at GoCardless Bank Account Data
  2. Get your credentials from User Secrets
  3. Add them to your .env file as GOCARDLESS_SECRET_ID and GOCARDLESS_SECRET_KEY
  4. Restart your Django server
